App Tutorial

Kaise use karein? / How to use?

1. Customer Profile (Sync)

Mobile number dalkar 'Sync Database' karein. Purana udhaar automatic aa jayega.
Enter mobile and sync to fetch past dues automatically.

2. Cart & Inventory

Items dalo aur rate set karo. Aap suggested items list se bhi select kar sakte hain.
Add items and rates. Use suggestions for faster entry.

3. GST Toggle (Tax)

Agar bill tax wala banana hai toh toggle ON rakhein, warna OFF kar dein.
Keep ON for tax invoices, OFF for regular slips.

4. Quick Pay (Part Payment)

Agar customer kuch paisa abhi de raha hai toh 'Quick Pay' mein dalkar 'Pay Part' karein.
Use Quick Pay for immediate partial or full payments via QR.

5. Generate & Share

Authorize button dabayein bill generate karne ke liye. Phir WhatsApp ya Image save karein.
Authorize to generate bill, then share on WhatsApp or save as PNG.
